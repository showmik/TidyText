**TidyText** is a fast, modern Windows application designed to clean, format, and enhance your plain text. Whether you need to fix messy spacing, normalize casing, or rewrite content using an AI Assistant, TidyText provides a responsive, user-friendly experience.

## Key Features

### ✨ AI Assistant
Enhance your writing with powerful AI models. 
* **Cloud & Local Support**: Connect to Anthropic, DeepSeek, Gemini, or OpenAI, or run completely local, privacy-first models using Ollama or LocalLM.
* **Built-in Templates**: Instantly rewrite, summarize, or reformat text using predefined prompts.
* **Real-time Suggestions**: See AI modifications seamlessly stream into your editor as they generate.
* **Persistent History**: Easily refer back to your previous interactions with the assistant.

### 🧹 Text Cleanup & Formatting
* **Trim Whitespace**: Remove stray spaces at the start and end of lines.
* **Collapse Blank Lines**: Eliminate excessive empty lines between paragraphs for a cleaner look.
* **Fix Punctuation Spacing**: Automatically normalize spaces around commas, periods, colons, quotes, and parentheses.
* **Smart Case Conversion**: Intelligently convert text to **Sentence case**, **Title Case**, **UPPERCASE**, or **lowercase**.

### 📊 Advanced Statistics & Readability
* **Live Metrics**: Instantly view word, character, sentence, paragraph, and line counts.
* **Readability Scores**: Analyzes your writing using the LIX readability formula to gauge text complexity.
* **Markdown Aware**: Accurately analyzes text while properly handling Markdown code blocks and formatting.

### 🎨 Modern Experience
* **Refined UI**: Enjoy a sleek interface with smooth animations, scalable typography, and robust light/dark theme support.
* **Undo & Redo**: Full keyboard shortcut support to quickly revert or reapply changes.

## Privacy First

TidyText respects your privacy. All traditional text cleaning and formatting tools operate **100% offline**. When using the AI Assistant, you have the freedom to choose your provider—including offline, local-only options like Ollama—ensuring your sensitive data never has to leave your machine.

## Installation

Grab the latest build from the repository’s **Releases** page, then run the installer.

## Usage

1. **Paste** your raw text into the TidyText editor.
2. **Clean**: Apply standard operations (like fixing spacing or changing case) from the toolkit.
3. **Enhance**: Open the AI Assistant to rewrite, refine, or summarize sections of your text.
4. **Copy** the finalized text to your clipboard to use anywhere.
